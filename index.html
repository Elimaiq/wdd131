Federal Republic of Nigeria

Nigeria, often referred to as the "Giant of Africa," is a country celebrated for its vibrant culture, diverse population, and economic potential. Located in West Africa, it is the most populous country on the continent, home to over 200 million people from more than 250 ethnic groups. As Africa's largest economy, Nigeria is a powerhouse in industries like oil and gas, technology, and agriculture. Its entrepreneurial spirit is evident in the rapid growth of its tech hubs, earning Lagos the nickname "Silicon Valley of Africa."
